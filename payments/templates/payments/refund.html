Refund & Return Policy

At Gana's Coffee Shop, we strive to provide the best experience for our customers. Below is our refund and return policy:

Refund Policy

If your payment has failed or you have a valid reason to request a refund, we offer a refund within 7-8 days of the payment date.

  • Refund requests must be made within 24 hours of the order.
  • Any refund request raised after one day will not be accepted.
  • Refunds will be processed back to the original payment method.
  • Processing time may take 5-7 business days.

Return Policy

Since we provide perishable food and beverages, returns are not accepted. However, if there is an issue with your order, such as a wrong or defective item, please contact our support team immediately.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our refund and return policy, feel free to contact us at chganapathi4225@gmail.com.
